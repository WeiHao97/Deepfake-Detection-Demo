Overview of Research

Columbia Researchers have developed an innovative system named Raidar (geneRative AI Detection viA Rewriting). This cutting-edge tool aims to identify whether text has been written by a human or generated by AI or large language models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, without the need for access to the model’s internal workings.

The core of Raidar's innovation lies in exploiting a unique trait of LLMs termed “stubbornness.” This trait describes LLMs' tendency to alter human-written text more readily than AI-generated text. LLMs often perceive AI-generated text as already optimal, resulting in minimal changes during rephrasing.

Ethics and Disclosure

The Raidar system (geneRative AI Detection viA Rewriting) developed by Columbia Researchers is provided for academic and research purposes only.
